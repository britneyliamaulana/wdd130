Welcome to WWR Rafting

We offer unforgettable white water rafting experiences on some of the most exciting rivers in the region. Our certified guides, commitment to safety, and love for the outdoors make every trip both thrilling and safe.

History

Founded in 2010, WWR Rafting began as a small group river enthusiasts with a passion for adventure and a deep respect for nature. What started as weekend excursions on local rivers has grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region. Over the years, we’ve expanded our routes, trained a team of certified guides, and introduced thousands of guests to the thrill of white water rafting. Our commitment to safety, sustainability, and unforgettable experiences has been the driving force behind our success and reputation..
