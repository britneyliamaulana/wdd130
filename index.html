Johannesburg, South Africa

Johannesburg, located in the southern country of Africa, is the capital city of the land. It is the largest city in the country and is a major economic powerhouse. Johannesburg is also known as the City Of Gold due to its origins in the gold mining industry. It also has my favourite amusement park named after its previous stance as a lovely gold mining site — Gold Reef City.
